Problems of the modern age

Current facts

  • More and more time is spent with the computer
  • People do not move enough because they use cars and other vehicles where no human power is used (hoverboard)
  • In urban areas, children do not know what kind of sporting activities are happening in the neightbourhood
  • Sport events are poorly visited both for participants and for viewers
  • There is no media coverage for simple events like walking/hiking in mountain trails

What will happen if this is builded ?

Some predictions

  • Kid interested in discovering new sports (like kayaking) can find kayak clubs in his city
  • Teenagers which are willing to complete marathon hiking can organize and do it toghether
  • Older swimmer can chime in nearby nonofficial swim events
  • Parents can join group walking with the kids to and from kindergarden
